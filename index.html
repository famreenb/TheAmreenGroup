Welcome

We are a research group at RISE, focusing on designing advanced materials for energy storage applications using computational approaches including Density functional theory (DFT), Molecular dynamics (MD), Machine learning methods etc.

Electrolyte Design

Modeling the non-aqueous liquid electrolyte to understand ion transport and interface composition.

Machine Learning Approach

Using machine learning potentials to design novel battery materials.

High-Entropy Materials

Exploring compositional complexity to develop next-gen battery electrodes with improved cycling.
